Business Context

Real-Time Business Scenario

The Revenue Management team at GrandStay Hotels faces critical challenges across their luxury portfolio.

📉
Inconsistent Occupancy Rates
Significant variance in room occupancy observed across properties, with some locations underperforming seasonally and requiring dynamic pricing intervention.
🍽️
Declining F&B Revenue
Food & Beverage revenue is declining at certain properties, requiring deeper analysis of spend patterns, menu performance, and upselling opportunities.
⭐
Poor Online Review Scores
Guest review scores below 6/10 at select properties are damaging brand reputation and directly impacting future booking conversions across channels.
